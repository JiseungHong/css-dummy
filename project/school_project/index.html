Abstract Text Summarizing Model for Title Generation

: Our team developed a Transformer-based model for generating article titles through abstract summarization.
The model comprises four key steps: data crawling, sentence extraction, model training, and style transfer.
These processes allowed us to fine-tune the Transformer model effectively, leading to satisfactory results measured by the BLEU score.
